How to use this app

Follow these steps to get your room in sync.

  1. 1
    Paste the video link.

    Works on most sites—drop the URL into ViTwo.

  2. 2
    Press “Detect Video.”

    ViTwo will read the stream info and show the duration.

  3. 3
    Check the video duration.

    If it shows 0:00, switch to the page view, play the video, and press Reload until the correct duration appears.

  4. 4
    Still stuck?

    Change a site or email us using the button above so we can investigate.
